Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before working with StudioPopCrimes.

How do I place an order?

Simply use the contact form on our website or send us a message via Instagram @studiopopcrimes to start your design request.

How long does it take to receive the design?

Most orders are completed within 2–4 days depending on complexity and queue. We always strive for both speed and quality.

What type of designs do you offer?

We specialize in clothing mockups, graphic merch, brand visuals, poster designs, and creative visuals tailored for fashion brands.

Can I request revisions?

Yes, we offer 1–2 revisions depending on the package. We want you to be fully satisfied with your final design.

Do you offer bundle packages for multiple designs?

Absolutely! If you need several pieces like a full drop set or brand kit, message us for bundle pricing and deals.

What’s your payment method?

We accept payments through PayPal or direct transfer. Full payment is required before final delivery.
